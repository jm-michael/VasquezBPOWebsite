SERVICES

Healthcare

We help healthcare organizations with requirements for finance and compliance-based consulting, reporting, and government mandated filings.

With healthcare’s crucial role in enhancing the quality of life through health, we understand how complex processes, accounting transactions, and evolving industry legal frameworks and regulations need not be overlooked. On top of their fundamental duty, these organizations must closely manage risk, and monitor costs, revenues, and grant program requirements. It takes insight and an experienced accounting partner to evaluate your organization’s efficiency and fiscal fitness for future operations. Vasquez has the industry experts who will embrace your vision and ease concerns in these essential areas. Organizations use the information we derive to evaluate reimbursement levels, budgeting for future operations and how your organization compares to other similar community health centers. We provide you with the best information and assistance available to keep your operations running more effectively.

Organizations we serve:

  • Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC)
  • Mental Health
  • Community Health Care
  • Managed Care

Employee Benefit Plan

Rest easy. We deliver quality benefit plan audits.

Employee Benefit Plans have unique financial reporting requirements and audit environment as they are governed by various laws and regulations. With our long-term experience providing service for over 40 employee benefit plans ranging from 100 to 40,000 participants with $100,000 to $6 billion in assets, we guarantee our expertise in the industry. We will ensure your compliance with the DOL and ERISA filing and SEC requirements and provide professional assistance with fiduciary responsibilities, plan qualifications, and reporting alternatives.

We assist in several areas and plans of types and sizes, including, but not limited to:

  • Defined Contribution Plan
  • Defined Benefit Plan
  • Health & Welfare
  • Tax Reporting: Form 5500
